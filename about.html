Who We Are

Intro

Agra Startups is an open group for Entrepreneurs and Aspiring Entrepreneurs. This is an effort to nurture an eco-system in our city.

With the inception of the idea, every entrepreneur have series of doubts related to company registration, finding a good team, raising money and many more. He/she strives hard to move in the right direction to make it big. Agra Startups Club aims to be an incubator that help entrepreneurs solve some of the problems commonly associated with running a startup by providing workspace, seed funding, mentoring, and training. Our sole purpose is to help you grow your business.
